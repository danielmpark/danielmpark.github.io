WOD

Fitness: 135/95
Performance: 185/125
Open: 225/155

Getting our pause work in with FS today. Then moving to a simple but extremely hard workout. If this is done right people should be on the ground after the work. The deadlift load should be done in 10's or 5 and 5 then they should jump right into the air squats.




Deadlifts should take about 20-25 minutes then you work into a met con that you need to have the room set up for. The walk should be about the length of the rig or a little longer. Everyone starts on one side with their ropes behind them. Swing, walk down then walk back and hit a max set of doubles. Morning crew set the length of the walk with cones and make it as long as possible so people have about 1 minutes on the doubles.
