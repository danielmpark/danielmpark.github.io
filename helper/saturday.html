Saturday

WOD

5 rounds

5 Ring Muscle ups or 10 pull ups + 5 Dips

20 DB Step ups

400 M run

25 min Cap


We have packed this week with strength, skill and core work. Today is open. USE it as a coach. The big movement to get people set up for is the Ring MU. You should have time today to help everyone preserve the stimulus. The MUs should take no longer than 1:00 each and every round. The cap is generous so people can take a little longer, but try not to let someone push for RX and take 3:00 minutes on one set of 5 MUs. That misses the point.
