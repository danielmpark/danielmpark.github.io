Saturday

Strength WOD

Take 12 minutes to
Build to a heavy 2 Rep Push Press


P/PP Mods:

No mods
Can opt for 4/4/4/4/4 at same (lighter) weight across


WOD

12 min AMRAP
4 HSPU
8 Pull ups
12 Alt Pistols


P/PP Mods:

Sub strict press off rack or w/ DBs for HSPU
Sub ring rows or j-hook rows for pull-ups
Pistols to a box


HSPU Scale- 1-arm Kneeling Arnold Press
Pistol Scale- 1-arm DB Front Squat (6 on L, 6 on R)
**use same DB for both movements

Working some pressing strength, directly into a technical 12 minute amrap. Should be something people are moving through. Heart rate should be moderate, but not totally jacked up today. Make sure people have progressions to fall back. 12 minutes is a long time and it will add up for people!
