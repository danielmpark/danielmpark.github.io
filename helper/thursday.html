WOD

For 25 minutes

0-8 min : 10/8 Assault Bike Cals + Max Pushups

P/PP Mod:Modified box pushups or sub box dips

Rest 8-10 min

10-18 min : 14 Alt DB Snatch + Low Plank Hold

P/PP Mod: Sub bird dogs for low plank

Rest 18-20 min

20-28 min : 14 DB Death March | (Youtube Demo Link)

P/PP Mod: Optional- sub sumo DL w/ DBs for death march


Max Single Arm DB Push Press (use one of the same DBs as the death march) Today is different, in a fun way! Set the class up by going through a few of these movements. Tons of points of performance to touch on and ways to warm people up. The BIG focus should be the HINGE position and keep peoples backs in a safe position for the snatch and the death march. Have fun with this one! It should leave people on he ground.
