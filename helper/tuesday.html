Trying something a little different today! Lets work on cycling. For a beginner or ANYONE who has trouble getting back to the ground in a safe position please have them go from the hang. Clean and jerks need to be unbroken today so people should choose their weight accordingly. Into a HARD sprint on the assault bike then 2 minutes of rest. This should be super hard, get the energy high and have people sprinting!
