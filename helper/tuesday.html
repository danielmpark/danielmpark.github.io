Tuesday

Skill WOD

Take 10 minutes to work on Muscle ups and or Pull ups
*See coaches notes

WOD

Every 3 minutes on 1 minute off for 20 minutes
___ Ring Muscle ups
10 Power Snatch
Max Doubles

Fitness: 10 pull ups + 10 Box Dips (Pullup Scale- Jhook Bodyweight rows), 95/65
Performance: 10 C2B Pullups + 5 Ring Dips, 115/80
Open: 5 Ring Muscle ups, 135/95

I left the skill work open today so the coach could take control of the exercises they want to do and read their class. If only 1 person is doing Muscle ups you can spend most of your time on the pull up. Or if you have 10 people that want to jump to the rings you can spend time there. Athletes should get to the doubles with about 1 minute of 1:30. The muscle ups should be at a place where people have to break them up in the later rounds. For fitness I have box dips in place instead of rings so they can get some good range of motion and move through them quickly.
